About Me

My name is Elfan, a self-taught UI Designer from Indonesia with 2 years of experience and still growing. My journey began in 2020 when everyone was required to stay at home due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

I used that time to explore myself, learning various skills such as coding and content creation, until I discovered the field I love and have been dedicated to ever since, UI design.

Throughout my journey, I have helped design websites, apps, and social media for clients ranging from local (Indonesia) to international (Germany, the UK, the US, and Argentina).
